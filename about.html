Pressing flowers is a craft for the sentimental.

Its physical preservation extends beyond the floral keepsakes. Pressing flowers can revive special occasions, gifts from loved ones, and many more memories that are meant to last.

The art of pressing flowers originates from feudal Japan, where Samurai would hone their harmony with nature by creating elaborate pictures from pressed botanical elements. They would preserve the fleeting florals into artifacts that lasted generations.

However, this craft is not limited to intricate creations; it can be as simple as an afternoon activity. Above, there are three steps to creating the perfect floral artifact.
